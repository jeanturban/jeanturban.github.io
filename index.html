About Me

I was born in Switzerland, grew up in Mexico, and later moved to Miami, Florida. Now I'm here: an undergrad at Caltech majoring in Mechanical Engineering and minoring in Computer Science. I have a heavy interest in robotics (which spurred the major/minor combo) and love being able to design and prototype an idea from all fronts. If you would like to know more about me academically, feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn or check out my resume.

When I am not solving problem sets and studying, I love nothing more than travelling, playing sports, and dabbling in my interests: I have backpacked the Swiss Alps, competed in triathlons, visited 11 countries, and created my own paintings. My current hobbies include algotrading, quadcopters, bouldering, and obsessively waiting for the World Cup.
